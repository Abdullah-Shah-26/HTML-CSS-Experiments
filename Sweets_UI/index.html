Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar with water to make syrup (add cardamom/rose essence if desired).
  2. Trim bread edges, soak in milk, and knead into smooth dough.
  3. Shape small balls and deep fry in medium-hot oil until golden brown.
  4. Soak fried balls in warm syrup for 30 minutes before serving.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and mix with milk powder.
  2. Prepare sugar syrup until slightly sticky and add to mixture.
  3. Cook until thick and leaves the sides of the pan.
  4. Pour into a greased plate, set, and cut into desired shapes.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Lemon juice / Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Boil milk and curdle it with lemon juice/vinegar to make chenna.
  2. Drain, wash, and knead chenna into a smooth dough.
  3. Shape into small balls and boil in sugar syrup until they expand.
  4. Serve chilled in syrup after resting.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Boil sugar with water to form a one-string syrup.
  3. Add cashew powder and cook until it thickens into dough.
  4. Roll, cut into diamond shapes, and garnish with silver leaf.
